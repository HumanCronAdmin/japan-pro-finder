# About Japan Pro Finder

Japan Pro Finder is a free directory that helps foreign residents in Japan find English-speaking professionals — lawyers, doctors, tax accountants, financial advisors, and real estate agents.

## Why We Built This

Finding a professional who speaks your language in Japan is hard. Embassy referral lists are short, Google results are hit-or-miss, and asking on Reddit gives you the same five recommendations.

We built Japan Pro Finder to be the comprehensive, up-to-date directory that we wished existed when we needed it.

## What We Do

- **Verify listings** using publicly available information
- **Categorize by specialty** so you find the right expert for your situation
- **Keep it free** — basic listings cost nothing, now and forever
- **Update regularly** based on user feedback and our own research

## How Listings Work

All listings are compiled from publicly available information. We do not charge for basic listings.

If you are a professional and want to update or remove your listing, email us at **contact@japanprofinder.com**.
