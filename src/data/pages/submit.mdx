# Submit Your Business

Japan Pro Finder helps foreign residents in Japan find English-speaking professionals. If you offer services in English, we'd love to feature you.

## Who Can Be Listed

- Lawyers (immigration, divorce, labor, corporate)
- Doctors and clinics with English-speaking staff
- Tax accountants serving foreign residents
- Financial advisors for expats
- Real estate agents helping foreigners
- Other professionals serving the international community

## What We Need From You

To create your listing, please send us:

1. **Business name**
2. **Category** (lawyer, doctor, tax accountant, etc.)
3. **Location** (city/ward)
4. **Website URL**
5. **Short description** (2-3 sentences about your services and English support)
6. **Specialties** (e.g., immigration visa, international divorce, NISA/iDeCo)

## How to Submit

Email the information above to:

**contact@japanprofinder.com**

We review submissions within 3 business days. Currently free during our launch period.

## Already Listed?

If you're already on Japan Pro Finder and want to update your information, email us at the address above with your business name and the changes you'd like to make.
