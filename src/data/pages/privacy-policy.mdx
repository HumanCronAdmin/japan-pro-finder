# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2, 2026*

## What We Collect

Japan Pro Finder is a static directory website. We do not require user accounts or collect personal information directly.

When you visit our site, basic technical data may be collected automatically:

- Pages visited and time spent
- Browser type and device information
- Referring website
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

This data is collected through analytics tools to help us understand how visitors use the site and improve our service.

## Directory Listings

Professional listings on Japan Pro Finder are compiled from publicly available information. If you are a listed professional and would like to:

- **Update your information**: Email us at japanprofinder@gmail.com
- **Remove your listing**: Email us and we will remove it within 3 business days

## Cookies

We may use cookies for basic site functionality such as remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode). We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Japan Pro Finder is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
